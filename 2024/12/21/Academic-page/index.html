Welcome to my academic page

Yibo Zhou

PhD Candidate, School of Earth Sciences and Engineering, Nanjing University

I work on sexual selection in Mesozoic birds and non-avian dinosaurs, with additional interests in how early feather functions (display, insulation, and flight) evolved.
